SafeScan Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Information Collection

SafeScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

Scan History

QR scan history is stored only on your device using iOS secure storage and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

SafeScan uses Google Web Risk API to check URLs for security threats. When you scan a QR code, the URL is sent to Google's servers for analysis. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No User Accounts

SafeScan does not require or support user accounts. No login information is collected.

Data Security

All scan history is encrypted and stored locally on your device. You can delete your history at any time within the app.

Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact Us
